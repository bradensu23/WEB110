Our Family Story

A Family Tradition of Service

Suffield's Heating, Cooling & Plumbing is a family-owned HVAC and plumbing company in Marion, KS, proudly serving Marion County and nearby areas for over 30 years. We provide expert air conditioning, furnace, heating, and plumbing services with a tradition of treating every customer like family and every project as a promise.

Our Story

Founded on integrity and a passion for helping our community, we started with small service calls and word-of-mouth referrals in Marion, KS. Through decades of hard work and continual learning, we've grown alongside Marion County, earning the trust of families and businesses alike with reliable HVAC and plumbing solutions.

We're not just service providers—we're your friends, neighbors, and fellow Kansans, committed to delivering comfort, safety, and peace of mind to every home.

Our Values

  • Honest, respectful communication
  • Attention to detail and safety
  • Giving back to our local community

We believe in standing behind our work, supporting our employees, and giving back whenever we can. That’s the Suffield way.
